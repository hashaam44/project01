A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

On October 15, 1931, APJ Abdul Kalam was born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He was born into a Tamil Muslim family. Abdul Kalam was the youngest of five siblings. Since he was a child, Kalam has always helped his family. He was captivated by studies, particularly mathematics, from a young age. During his school years, Kalam was recognized as a brilliant and dedicated student.Dr. Kalam's childhood was difficult because his family's business failed and they lost all income sources. He was born into a poor family, but he overcame all obstacles and worked tirelessly throughout his life to achieve his dream. He started selling newspapers when he was young to help support his family. Despite his average grades, his teachers saw him as a hardworking student with an inquiring mind.
